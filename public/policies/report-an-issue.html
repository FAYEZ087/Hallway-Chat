Report an Issue

If you've encountered inappropriate behavior, technical issues, or any other problems, we want to know.

How to Report

During a Call

Tap the Report button on screen, choose a reason, and send it. Our moderation team will be notified instantly.

After a Call

Email safety@hallwaychat.online with the time of the call and a description of what happened.

Technical Issues

For bugs, camera/mic issues, or platform glitches, email support@hallwaychat.online with your browser information and a description of the issue.

What Happens Next

  • Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours.
  • Your identity is always kept anonymous from the reported user.
  • Confirmed violations result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans.
  • We will email you once action has been taken.
