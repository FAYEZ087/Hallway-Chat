Community

Hallway is made by students, for students. We believe that college is better when you have a strong network and friends who understand the journey.

Our Mission

College is easier when you know more people. Use Hallway to find a study buddy, a new friend, or someone to talk to during those late-night hostel hours. We aim to break the barriers of departments and campuses, connecting India's student body in one safe space.

Campus Ambassadors

Want Hallway on your campus? Join our ambassador program! As an ambassador, you'll help grow the community at your college, organize local events, and get early access to upcoming features. It's a great way to build leadership skills and meet fellow student leaders.
