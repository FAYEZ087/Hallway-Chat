Platform Features

Hallway helps verified students meet and talk without the usual spam or fake accounts. Here's what you can do:

🛡️
Verified-Only Access You need a valid .edu, .ac.in, or .in email to join. Every user is a real student.
🎯
Interest-Based Matching Pick your interests and get matched with people who like similar things.
📹
HD Video Chat Fast 1-on-1 calls with crystal clear audio and high-definition video.
📖
Study Together Mode Find someone to study with and stay focused on your goals.
📍
Campus Filters Match within your specific campus or across colleges nationwide.
🦉
Night Owl Badge Earn exclusive badges if you are active during late-night study sessions.
🚀
Events & Hackathons Stay updated with student-run events and hackathons happening around you.
🔥
Streaks & Badges Keep a daily streak and unlock rewards as you engage with the community.
