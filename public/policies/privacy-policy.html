1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us

In Short: We collect personal information that you provide to us.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Services, set up your profile, or otherwise contact us.

Personal Information Provided by You. The personal information we collect may include the following:

  • College or university email address (used for verification)
  • Display name or nickname
  • Real name (stored privately, not shown to other users unless you choose)
  • College or university name
  • Profile photo (uploaded to our secure storage)
  • Interests and topics selected during profile setup

Sensitive Information. We do not process sensitive personal information.

Email Authentication. Hallway uses magic link authentication. When you enter your college email, we send a one-time login link via email. We do not store passwords. Authentication is handled securely via Supabase.

Video & Audio Calls. All video and audio communication on Hallway is peer-to-peer via WebRTC (using the simple-peer library and metered.ca TURN servers for connection routing). We do not record, store, intercept, or have access to the content of your video or audio calls.

Chat Messages. Text messages sent during a video call session are transmitted in real time via Socket.io. We do not persistently store chat message content after a session ends.

All personal information you provide must be true, complete, and accurate, and you must notify us of any changes.

2. HOW DO WE PROCESS YOUR INFORMATION?

In Short: We process your information to provide and improve our Services, verify your student status, match you with other users, and ensure safety on the platform.

We process your personal information for the following reasons:

  • To verify your student status. We use your college email address to confirm you are a current student at a recognized institution. Only verified accounts can use Hallway.
  • To facilitate account creation and authentication. We process your information so you can create and log in to your account via magic link.
  • To match you with other students. We use your selected interests and availability to find relevant matches through our real-time matching system.
  • To display your profile. Your display name, profile photo, college, and interests may be shown to matched users during a session.
  • To respond to user inquiries and support requests. We process your information to respond to reports, complaints, and support queries.
  • To enforce our community guidelines. We may process information related to reported users to investigate violations and take appropriate action including bans.
  • To send administrative communications. We may send you emails related to your account, safety updates, or policy changes.
  • To evaluate and improve our Services. We may analyze usage patterns to improve matching, performance, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations. We may process your information to comply with applicable laws, legal requests, or to establish, exercise, or defend legal rights.
3. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

In Short: We may share information in specific situations with specific third parties.

We use the following third-party services to operate Hallway:

  • Supabase — for authentication, database storage, and profile photo storage.
  • Resend — for sending magic link authentication emails.
  • metered.ca — for TURN server services that help establish peer-to-peer video connections. metered.ca routes connection metadata but does not have access to call content.
  • Vercel — for hosting the frontend application.
  • Render — for hosting the backend server.

We may also share information in the following situations:

  • Business Transfers. We may share or transfer your information in connection with any merger, sale of assets, financing, or acquisition of all or part of our business.
  • Legal Requirements. We may disclose your information where required by law, regulation, or valid legal process (such as a court order or government request).
  • Safety. We may share information where we believe it is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Hallway, our users, or others.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information for advertising purposes.

4. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

In Short: We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services or as required by law.

We will retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable period, unless retention is required by law (such as to prevent fraud, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations).

Session data (such as real-time chat messages) is not stored persistently after a session ends. Profile information (name, display name, college, photo, interests) is retained until you delete your account.

5. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?

In Short: We use technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information.

We have implemented appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Row-level security (RLS) policies on our database so users can only access their own data
  • Magic link authentication — no passwords stored
  • Peer-to-peer WebRTC for video and audio — we cannot access call content
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Rate limiting and input validation on our backend server

However, no electronic transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your information, you access the Services at your own risk.

6. DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION FROM MINORS?

In Short: We do not knowingly collect data from or market to children under 18 years of age.

Hallway is intended for use by college and university students who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or process data from anyone under 18. By using the Services, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. If we learn that we have collected personal information from someone under 18, we will deactivate that account and delete the data promptly. If you become aware of any such case, please contact us at safety@hallwaychat.online.
